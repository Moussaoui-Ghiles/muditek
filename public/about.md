## Methodology — Diagnose. Build. Own.

### 1. Diagnose
We pull your data, map your workflows, and quantify every hour of waste. In euros, with the formulas. You see exactly where the money goes before any system is built. If we can't find meaningful waste, the diagnostic is free.

### 2. Build
We build the AI system that eliminates what the diagnostic found. Custom-built for your operations, on your infrastructure. You own the code, the data, and the system. No SaaS fees, no vendor lock-in.

### 3. Own
You own everything we build. Complete source code, your database, your infrastructure. We offer an optional retainer for optimization and scaling, but the system runs whether you keep us or not.
