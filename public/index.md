## Methodology

1. **Diagnose first, build second.** Every engagement starts with a diagnostic. No code until both sides agree on the size and shape of the waste — in euros, with formulas. If we can't find meaningful waste, you don't pay.
2. **Reuse the knowledge, not the code.** Multi-jurisdiction compliance, on-premises AI deployment, revenue ops automation — these problems are already solved. We adapt proven architectures to your specifics. That's why we deliver in weeks what dev shops quote 12-18 months for.
3. **Client owns everything.** Source code, database, infrastructure, data — all yours. No SaaS subscriptions, no per-seat licensing, no vendor lock-in.
