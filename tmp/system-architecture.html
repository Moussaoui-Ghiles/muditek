§ 04

Data layer · what's actually stored

Twelve tables in Neon. Where every signup, payment, lead, and email event lives.

Table Rows Purpose Health
newsletter_subscribers 5,365 Free email list. HOT 2,002 · WARM 998 · COLD 2,364. Token-based unsub + topic prefs. Healthy
subscribers 0 Paying $47/mo customers. Linked to Stripe customer + Clerk user. Empty
newsletter_issues drafts Compose / schedule / send newsletter editions. Markdown body + audience filter. Idle since Apr 20
newsletter_events log Per-recipient send / open / click / bounce events from Resend webhooks. Healthy
content_items 14 / 0 Library. Free playbooks (14) shown to all portal users. Paid items (0) gated to subscribers. Paid shelf empty
campaigns 4 LinkedIn lead-magnet campaigns. Each has keyword + landing URL + expiry. All expired
commenters log LinkedIn comments scraped by Apify. Matched to campaigns by keyword. Dormant
submissions log Form fills on /c/[id] landing pages. Verified flag once keyword matches. Dormant
deliveries log Lead-magnet email sends. One row per asset delivered. Dormant
sequence_sends log Nurture drip queue. Pending / sent / failed steps per lead. No content
drops log Monthly content drops to paid subscribers (kit refresh). Unused
email_log log All outbound email events from Resend (welcome, drop, sequence, newsletter). Healthy
§ 05

Where it leaks

Five places money or trust falls out of the system today. Ranked by impact.

Leak 01 · biggest
Newsletter is silent
5,365 subs. Last newsletter_issue with status='sent': Apr 20. List-warmth + open-rate compounds against you weekly.
Leak 02
Paid shelf is empty
/buy charges $47/mo. content_items.is_free=false count = 0. Selling vapor.
Leak 03
Portal unknown to subs
clerk_user_id IS NOT NULL on newsletter_subscribers: 1 / 5,365. Free portal exists, nobody's been told.
Leak 04
LinkedIn front door closed
0 active campaigns. Apify cron runs daily but has nothing to scrape. Highest-velocity acquisition channel is dark.
Leak 05
Auth UX leaks vendor
Clerk dev instance shows "Development mode" banner + "Secured by Clerk" footer on every auth page. 3 dashboard toggles fix it.
