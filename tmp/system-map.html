Where money is leaking

§ 03 · ranked by pain

Four leaks. All cost you money. Ranked by how much and how fast.

Leak 01 · the big one
Silent newsletter
5,365 people said "yes, email me." You haven't sent in 4 days. Every silent week kills your open rate. This list is the asset you already earned — not using it is the single biggest loss in the business today.
Leak 02
Selling an empty shelf
Your $47/month checkout is live. Stripe is wired. If a stranger pays today, they log into the paid portal and see nothing inside. Better to hide the Buy button than to sell empty.
Leak 03
Nobody knows the portal exists
5,364 subscribers trust you enough to be on your list. They'd take a free account if asked. Right now no email mentions that the portal even exists. One CTA in the next newsletter fixes it.
Leak 04
LinkedIn front door is closed
Your biggest traffic channel. Your proven lead-capture play (comment a keyword, get a playbook) is not running. One post with a keyword turns the whole capture & nurture machine back on.

Three moves fix most of it.

Not new features. Not more code. Just turning on machines that already exist.

Tonight
Write one newsletter, send to the hot 2,002.
Put "Create your free Muditek account →" in the footer. One email kicks on three things at once: newsletter cadence restarts, people start claiming accounts, and the portal starts getting traffic for the first time.
This week
Decide on the paid kit.
Either seed 3–5 real paid items in the library so $47 is worth paying, or hide the Buy button from the site until you're ready. Advertising an empty store burns trust worse than no store at all.
Next LinkedIn post
Turn the front door back on.
One post with a keyword + a lead magnet. The scrape → email → playbook → drip machine catches everything automatically. You just need to publish again.
